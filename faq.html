Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our electric fleet and services.

Home / FAQ
Are all your vehicles electric?

Yes! We are proud to have a 100% electric fleet. Our mission is to save nature by reducing carbon emissions with every ride.

How do I charge the rented EV?

All our rental vehicles come fully charged. For longer trips, our app shows you the nearest charging stations. We also have partnerships with major charging networks for free or discounted charging.

How do I book a cab?

You can easily book a cab through our website or mobile app. Just enter your pickup and drop location, choose your preferred EV, and confirm your ride.

What documents do I need to rent a vehicle?

To rent a self-drive EV, you need a valid driving license, a government ID proof (Aadhar/Passport), and a small security deposit which is refundable.

Is it safe to travel in EVs?

Absolutely. Our EVs are modern, regularly serviced, and safety-tested. Plus, our verified drivers ensure a secure and comfortable journey.
